Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you visit FaceTea Shop, we may collect:

  • Personal information: Name, email address, shipping address, and phone number when you place an order or contact us.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe and/or PayPal. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, and browser type via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Send promotional emails (only if you subscribe; you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Improve our website and product offerings

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) to process transactions
  • Shipping carriers to deliver your orders
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics) to understand site usage

4. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your cart, understand site traffic, and improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site features may not work properly.

5. Data Security

We protect your information with 256-bit SSL encryption. Payment data is handled entirely by PCI-compliant processors (Stripe/PayPal).

6. Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your personal information at any time by emailing support@facetea.org.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, email us at support@facetea.org.
