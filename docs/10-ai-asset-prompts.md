# 10 — AI Asset Prompts

## Consistency Prompt Header

Prepend this block to **every** image prompt to maintain visual consistency across all assets:

```
CONSISTENCY HEADER (paste at the start of every image prompt):

Style: editorial lifestyle photography, soft diffused natural light from the left,
warm color grade (+10 temperature, slightly matte/film-look, desaturated highlights),
clean white (#FFFFFF) or warm off-white (#F7F5F2) background,
minimal props (max 3: ceramic tray, small potted plant, folded linen towel),
no people's faces visible OR diverse skin tones only (no specific celebrity or recognizable person),
aspect ratio as specified per prompt,
do NOT reference any trademarked brand, logo, or copyrighted character.
```

## 5 Short Video Storyboard Prompts (15–25 seconds)

Use with **Runway Gen-3**, **Pika Labs**, **Kling AI**, or **Sora** when available.  
Each prompt includes scene description, camera movement, and audio cue.

---

### Video 1 — "The Pod Dissolves" (Satisfying Hook)

```
AI VIDEO PROMPT:
Scene: Extreme close-up of a small oval pod resting in a wet palm (medium-brown skin, no face visible).
The pod begins to dissolve as water contacts it, creating expanding white foam bubbles.
Camera: slow push-in macro, very slight pull-back as foam grows.
Duration: 15 seconds.
Color: warm, soft diffused natural light, high contrast on the foam texture.
Audio cue: gentle water drip sound, then soft ambient piano note (no narration needed).
End frame: foam filling the palm, pod fully dissolved.
Style: satisfying, ASMR-adjacent, clean beauty aesthetic.
Do NOT include any copyrighted music, brand references, or celebrity likeness.
```
