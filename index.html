Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

All our formulas are dermatologically tested and free of parabens, sulfates (SLS/SLES), and artificial dyes. We recommend a patch test on the inside of your wrist if you have known sensitivities. Our Chamomile Calming Face Wash is specifically designed for sensitive and irritation-prone skin.

Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?

Yes! Every FaceTea product is 100% vegan and cruelty-free. We never test on animals and use no animal-derived ingredients.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) and PayPal. All transactions are encrypted via SSL and processed securely by Stripe or PayPal — we never see or store your full card number.

Can I change or cancel my order?

We can modify or cancel orders within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order has typically been sent to fulfillment. Email support@facetea.org immediately if you need a change.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 7–14 business days (free on orders $50+, otherwise $4.99). Expedited shipping takes 3–5 business days ($7.99). All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. You'll receive a tracking number by email.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. Unopened products can be returned for a full refund or exchange. Opened products qualify for 50% store credit. Damaged, defective, or wrong items are replaced free of charge. If you experience a skin sensitivity reaction, we'll issue a full refund — no return required.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?

Yes! For orders of 10 or more of any product, email support@facetea.org with "Wholesale Inquiry" in the subject line.

What skincare routine do you recommend?

For a complete FaceTea routine: 1) Cleanse with Chamomile Face Wash, 2) Tone with Black Tea Firming Toner, 3) Treat with Green Tea Brightening Serum, 4) Moisturise with Oolong Hydrating Day Cream (AM) or Rooibos Repair Night Oil (PM). Add the White Tea Eye Cream and use the Matcha Detox Clay Mask 1–2x per week. Exfoliate with Hibiscus AHA Pads 2–3x per week.
